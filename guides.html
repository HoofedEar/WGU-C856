FAQ

What kind of power outlets are used on Taniti?
120 volt outlets (same as in the United States).

What is the legal drinking age?
The legal drinking age on Taniti is 18. Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.

Are there healthcare facilities on the island?
There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

What kind of currency is accepted on the island?
Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
